# Contributing to QA Engineer AI Career Resource

Thank you for using this resource. It is maintained by Sriram Advisory and updated as the Indian QA job market evolves.

## How to Contribute

### Report an Issue
If you find outdated information, broken links, or factual errors:
1. Open a GitHub Issue
2. Describe what is wrong and where it appears
3. Share the correct information if you have it

### Suggest an Addition
If you want to suggest a new section, tool, project brief, or resource:
1. Open a GitHub Issue with the label `enhancement`
2. Describe what you want added and why it would help QA engineers

### Share Your Experience
If you used this repo and landed a role — your story could help others:
1. Open a GitHub Issue with the label `success-story`
2. Share (anonymously if preferred) what worked and what you would change
3. We may feature your transition pattern as an illustrative example

## What We Do Not Accept
- Promotional content for specific companies or products
- Content that is not specific to Indian QA engineers
- Unverified salary claims without sourcing
