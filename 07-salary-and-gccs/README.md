## LinkedIn Search Strings That Work

AI Quality roles are posted under inconsistent titles. Use Boolean search to find all variants:

```
# General AI Quality
"quality engineer" OR "SDET" AI OR "machine learning" OR "LLM"

# BFSI specific
"quality" "AI" OR "machine learning" BFSI OR banking OR fintech Bangalore OR Hyderabad

# Playwright specific
"automation engineer" OR "SDET" Playwright OR "test automation" AI

# Senior / Architect
"quality architect" OR "quality lead" AI OR "LLM" OR "generative AI"
```

**Three LinkedIn search tips:**
1. Set 'Date Posted' to 'Past month' — roles older than 6 weeks are usually filled
2. Follow target company pages and turn on job alerts for 'quality'
3. Connect with QA leads and hiring managers at target companies before applying — warm applications have significantly higher response rates

## The Hiring Funnel Reality

```
100 applications submitted
        ↓ Recruiter screening (filtered: no portfolio, wrong tool keywords)
15 recruiter callbacks
        ↓ Technical screen (filtered: cannot explain Playwright/DeepEval)
5 interview rounds
        ↓ System design + behavioural
2 offers
        ↓ Negotiation
1 accepted
```

**Where most engineers are eliminated:** The first arrow. Not because they are unqualified — because their profile says "Selenium, Java, TestNG" and the recruiter is searching for "Playwright, Python, AI quality."

The filter is automated. You never get a chance to explain.

Two things pass the first filter:
1. The right keywords in your LinkedIn headline
2. A public GitHub repo with AI quality projects
